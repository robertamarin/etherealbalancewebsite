More Than Fitness.
It's Intentional.

Ethereal Balance was founded by Jazmin Sabah with a deeply personal mission: to create spaces where wellness becomes a bridge that connects people. Driven by her passion for bringing communities together through mindful movement, Jazmin envisioned something beyond traditional fitness—a movement rooted in intention.

What began in 2024 as intimate gatherings has blossomed into partnerships with leading hotels and corporations across Los Angeles. But at its core, Ethereal Balance remains true to Jazmin's original vision: wellness that's accessible, community-driven, and transformative.

Every event we host is designed with intention. Every movement has purpose. Every connection matters. This isn't just about exercise—it's about creating moments where people can disconnect from the noise and reconnect with themselves and each other.
